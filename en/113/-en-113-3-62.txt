NO. 62: THE SENATE 374

Qualifications of senators—Appointment of senators by State legislatures—Equality of representation in Senate—Mixed character of federal government requires a mixed representation—Equal representation in Senate a recognition of sovereignty in the States—The number of senators and the term for which they are elected—Need of such an upper house as a security against improper legislation, against the infirmity of faction, against ignorant legislation, against mutability in its councils from frequent changes of members—Dangers of such mutability enumerated.
