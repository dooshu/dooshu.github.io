NO. 72: THE SAME SUBJECT CONTINUED, AND RE-ELIGIBILITY OF THE EXECUTIVE CONSIDERED 434

Duration in office affects stability of administration—Heads of department dependent on Executive and will change with him—Re-eligibility of Executive—The opposition to it considered—A limit of a single term would diminish inducements to good behavior, increase temptations to misconduct, prevent experience in the office, deprive the country in emergencies of the services of the best men, and act as a constitutional barrier to stability of administration—The supposed advantages of a single term considered—The people should not be prevented from choosing men of experience.
